What If Rocks...

Rocks are strong

On the outside and never

Ever

Let anyone in.

All of them

Special

All of them

Beautiful

In one way or another, and never

Ever

Alone.

What if

On the inside,

They hurt despite their beauty

They were lonely despite their companions

They felt pain

Even though rocks shouldn't feel

Any emotion at all.

What if...

They need someone to rest by

They need some support

Even though rocks always provide those things and never

Ever

Need them.

What if

Those people

Who are our rocks,

Need someone too.

Sometimes all they need is a moment...

A person,

A friend

Who will just be there,

And let them know that

They are never

Ever

On their own.
